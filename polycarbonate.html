About Polycarbonate Roofing Sheets

Polycarbonate roofing sheets are lightweight, highly durable, and resistant to extreme weather conditions. They allow natural light to pass through while providing excellent protection from UV rays. These sheets are ideal for greenhouses, carports, verandas, and modern roofing designs.
